Senior citizens face overwhelming complexity navigating fragmented benefits systems (Social Security, Medicare A/B/C/D, SNAP, SSI, LIHEAP), healthcare logistics, and rising fraud threats.

The Solution

SilverLine deploys four specialized AI agents working in concert:

  • Benefits Enrollment Agent: Screens for 30+ federal/state/local benefits, guides applications, manages renewals
  • Medicare Plan Optimizer Agent: Analyzes coverage options annually, projects costs based on personal health data
  • Appointment & Ride Scheduler Agent: Coordinates medical logistics, books transportation, prevents missed appointments
  • Scam Shield Agent: Detects fraud patterns in real-time, verifies communications, provides warnings

Technology Stack: GPT-4/LangChain AI agents, integrated with government APIs (SSA, CMS, Benefits.gov) via MCP protocol. HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified.
